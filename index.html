Recipe #1

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 2-3

Step 1:in pan heat 2 tsp oil and saute 2 clove garlic and ½ onion.

Step 2:add ½ carrot, 2 tbsp capsicum, ½ tomato and 2 tbsp sweet corn.

Step 3:saute until the vegetables turn crunchy.

Step 4:further add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ¼ tsp salt and saute until the spices turn aromatic.

Step 5:add 1 cup water and get to a boil.

Step 6:now bread 1 pack maggi noodles roughly and add 1 pack tastemaker.

Step 7:mix well and boil for 2 minutes or until the noodles are cooked well.

Step 8:finally, add 2 tbsp coriander and enjoy masaledar maggi.
